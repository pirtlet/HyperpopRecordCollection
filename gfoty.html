About
GFOTY, an abbreviation of Girlfriend of the Year, is a British pop singer a part of the PC Music label who pokes fun at capitalism and big companies like Starbucks through her jarring, punk maximalist music. GFOTY often collaborates with A.G. Cook and Danny L Harle who are founders of the PC Music Label and leaders in cultivating the hyperpop sound.
